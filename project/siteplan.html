Site Name

Arsenal Fan Hub

The Arsenal Fan Hub serves as the ultimate digital destination for Arsenal Football Club supporters worldwide. This name was chosen because it perfectly encapsulates the community-driven nature of the site while clearly identifying its focus on Arsenal FC. The word "Hub" suggests a central gathering place where fans can access everything they need - from live match updates to player statistics and community interactions.

Optional Domain: arsenal-fan-hub.com or gunners-hub.org

Site Purpose

The Arsenal Fan Hub is designed to be a comprehensive digital platform that serves the global Arsenal fanbase by providing real-time match information, detailed player statistics, interactive community features, and essential club updates. The site aims to enhance the fan experience by offering:

  • Live Match Experience: Real-time countdowns, live scores, and match predictions
  • Player & Team Analytics: Comprehensive statistics, formation builders, and player comparisons
  • Community Engagement: Fan polls, feedback systems, and interactive discussions
  • Personalized Experience: Customizable preferences, favorite player tracking, and personalized dashboards
  • Mobile-First Design: Optimized for match-day viewing on all devices

Scenarios

These scenarios represent typical questions and needs of our target audience - passionate Arsenal fans:

Scenario 1: Match Day Preparation

"When is the next Arsenal match, and how can I prepare for it?"

A fan wants to know upcoming fixture details, predicted lineups, opponent analysis, and set reminders for the match. They also want to participate in pre-match polls and predictions.

Scenario 2: Player Performance Analysis

"How has our new signing been performing this season compared to other players in his position?"

Fans want detailed statistics, performance comparisons, and visual representations of player data to understand individual and team performance trends.

Scenario 3: Historical Match Information

"What was Arsenal's record against this opponent in recent seasons?"

Users seek historical match data, head-to-head records, and trends to better understand upcoming fixtures and team performance patterns.

Scenario 4: Community Engagement

"What do other Arsenal fans think about the latest transfer rumors?"

Fans want to engage with the community through polls, discussions, and shared opinions on club matters, transfers, and match performances.

  • Arsenal Gold: Accent borders, highlighting active states, success messages, and decorative elements
  • Arsenal Navy: Secondary headings, navigation elements, and body text alternatives
  • Supporting Colors: Light grays for backgrounds, dark gray for body text, and white for content areas

Typography

The typography system uses three carefully selected Google Fonts that complement Arsenal's brand identity while ensuring excellent readability across all devices:

Oswald - Primary Headings

Used for: Main page titles, hero headings, and prominent calls-to-action. This bold, condensed font captures the energy and passion of football.

Roboto Slab - Secondary Headings

Used for: Section headings, subheadings, and important labels. The slab serif adds authority and readability for structured content.

Open Sans - Body Text & UI Elements

Used for: All body text, navigation, forms, and user interface elements. This highly legible sans-serif ensures excellent readability on all devices.

Typography Hierarchy:

  • H1: Oswald, 2.5rem, Arsenal Red, Uppercase
  • H2: Roboto Slab, 1.8rem, Arsenal Navy
  • H3: Roboto Slab, 1.3rem, Arsenal Red
  • Body: Open Sans, 1rem, Dark Gray
  • Navigation: Open Sans, 0.9rem, White/Navy

Technical Implementation Notes

This site plan will guide the development of a fully functional Arsenal Fan Hub featuring:

  • Responsive CSS Grid/Flexbox: For adaptive layouts across all devices
  • JavaScript Interactivity: Match countdowns, polls, dynamic content loading
  • Local Storage: User preferences, favorite players, viewing history
  • Progressive Enhancement: Core functionality available without JavaScript
  • Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 compliant design with proper semantic markup
  • Performance Optimization: Optimized images, minified CSS/JS, fast loading times
